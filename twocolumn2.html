My Favorite Korean Drama

My First First Love (Available on NETFLIX)

My First First Love on Netflix (첫사랑은 처음이라서) tells the story of five college students and their messy encounters wiht the concept of their first love. Yoon Tae-oh is a college student whose friends — a college drop-out, a runaway family friend, and his long-time childhood friend — decide to unexpectedly move into his house, due their own individual reasonings. Now they must all learn to live together and learn to love.
